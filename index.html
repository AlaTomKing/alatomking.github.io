what does 'alatomking' stand for?

the name came from minecraft pocket edition in late 2016 because i tried to make my name green and bold i was called alatom without the king
it all started when i found a youtube tutorial on how to change the color of the signs
and i thought i could change the color of my name too
the a in ala makes the text green if u add dis symbol § in front of it the l makes the text bold
i added another a after the l on accident its supposed to be a green bold tom but it showed up like alatom without this symbol § i also think i made the first a capital idk why

my input (i think):
§A§l§aTom

expected result:
Tom

actual result:
AlaTom

the name 'alatom' came completly on accident and i decided to keep it

i played roblox at early 2017 because i was recommended a roblox version of thelegend27 meme (i thought it was legos at first).
